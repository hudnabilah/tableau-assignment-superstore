## üìä Key Insights

### A. Regional Sales & Profit
* [cite_start]**East** and **West** regions consistently show higher sales volumes[cite: 82].
* [cite_start]The **South** region has significant profit losses, notably in sub-categories like Tables and Machines[cite: 82, 83].
* [cite_start]The **Central** region is balanced but lacks significant growth[cite: 84].
* 
### B. Sales Trends Over Time
* [cite_start]Sales and profits follow a **seasonal trend**, peaking during **Q3 and Q4** (especially September to December)[cite: 86].
* [cite_start]Years like 2014 and 2015 showed sharper profit growth compared to 2013, which had uneven profitability[cite: 87].
* 
### C. Product Performance
* [cite_start]**Technology** products like **Copiers and Phones** are top performers in both sales and profitability[cite: 89].
* [cite_start]**Tables** in the **Furniture** category show high sales but **negative profits**, indicating potential issues with pricing or cost[cite: 90].
* [cite_start]No Furniture items appeared in the Top 10 most profitable products list[cite: 95].
